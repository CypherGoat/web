### Recent News

- WebWipe and MoneroTalk are hosting a Monero holiday meetup in New York City, New York, on December 19, from 5-10pm. [Details](https://luma.com/zoc6lubi)
- The MAGIC Monero Fund has opened a crowedfunding round for a second round of implementing fuzz testing in the Monero wallet, P2P layer, and FCMP++ upgrade code. [Details](https://donate.magicgrants.org/monero/projects/fuzzing-monero-2)
- omarespejel has released a prototype implementation of a trustless atomic swap protocol between Monero and Starknet. [Repository](https://github.com/omarespejel/monero-starknet-atomic-swap)
- Version v5.6.2 of Cake Wallet and the Monero.com wallets have been released. It includes bug fixes and improvements. [Release](https://github.com/cake-tech/cake_wallet/releases/tag/v5.6.2)
- Version v0.1.9 of monero-named-pipes has been released by d4ndox. It includes minor changes. [Release](https://github.com/d4ndox/mnp/releases/tag/0.1.9)
- The next Cuprate meeting has been scheduled for December 16, 2025 at 1800 UTC. It will take place in the [Matrix](https://matrix.to/#/#cuprate:monero.social)/[IRC-Libera](irc://irc.libera.chat/#cuprate) chats, in the #cuprate channel.
