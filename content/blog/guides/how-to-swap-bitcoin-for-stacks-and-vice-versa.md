## BTC↔STX swap flow

1. Select pair and amount
2. Compare route options
3. Confirm destination details
4. Send source asset
5. Monitor execution until completion

## What to check before executing

### Quote quality
Compare multiple providers, not only one quote source.

### Total cost
Include network assumptions and potential execution drift.

### Minimum receive
This is your practical downside boundary.

### Reliability
Prefer routes with better completion consistency over tiny quote edges.

## Common BTC↔STX mistakes

- stale quote usage
- wrong chain/address details
- no saved order/tracking IDs
- poor timing in high volatility windows

## FAQ

### Is BTC to STX swap always one-step?
User experience may feel one-step, but execution can involve multiple operational stages under the hood.

### How do I get the best BTC to STX rate?
Compare providers at the same amount/time and optimize for net receive after all costs.

### Should I split larger swaps?
For larger sizes, splitting can reduce slippage risk depending on route conditions.
