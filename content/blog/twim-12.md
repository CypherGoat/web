### Recent News

- monerobull has made a public service announcement regarding targeted FUD campaign against open-source P2P DEXs. [Announcement](https://farside.link/redlib/r/Monero/comments/1p5rvwe/)
- Version v0.0.8 of Cuprate has been released. It includes [killswitch removal](https://github.com/Cuprate/cuprate/pull/365), bug fixes, and improvements. Wallet sync is planned to be included in v0.1.0, noted as the first beta release. [Release](https://github.com/Cuprate/cuprate/releases/tag/cuprated-0.0.8)
- Version v5.6.0 of Cake Wallet and the Monero.com wallets have been released. It includes bug fixes, improvements, and better air-gap support. [Release](https://github.com/cake-tech/cake_wallet/releases/tag/v5.6.0)
- Version v6.25.11 of PiNodeXMR has been released by shermand100. It includes various bug fixes and improvements. [Release](https://github.com/shermand100/PiNodeXMR/releases/tag/v.6.25.11)
- The next Cuprate meeting has been scheduled for December 2, 2025 at 1800 UTC. It will take place in the [Matrix](https://matrix.to/#/#cuprate:monero.social)/[IRC-Libera](irc://irc.libera.chat/#cuprate) chats, in the #cuprate channel.
- The next Monero Tech meeting has been scheduled for December 1, 2025 at 1800 UTC. It will take place in the [Matrix](https://matrix.to/#/#no-wallet-left-behind:monero.social)/[IRC-Libera](irc://irc.libera.chat/#no-wallet-left-behind) chats, in the #no-wallet-left-behind channel.
