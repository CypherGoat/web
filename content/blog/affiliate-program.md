The CypherGoat affiliate program allows people and businesses to refer users to CypherGoat and earn a share of the revenue generated from completed swaps.

It exists to share revenue with partners who send relevant traffic to the platform.

The program is not an advertising network and is not designed for spam-driven promotion.

## **What the Affiliate Program Is**

The CypherGoat affiliate program allows partners to earn a percentage of the revenue generated from swaps that originate through their referral link.

Affiliates receive:
- a unique referral link
- access to an affiliate dashboard
- visibility into swap volume and earnings generated through their link

Users who swap through an affiliate link still interact with CypherGoat exactly the same way as any other user.


## **Who the Program Is For**

The affiliate program works best for anyone operating a website, tool, or community related to cryptocurrency.

It is designed for partners who already have audiences interested in cryptocurrency swaps.

It is **not intended for mass advertising campaigns or automated traffic schemes**.


## **How to Become an Affiliate**

Anyone interested in becoming a CypherGoat affiliate can create an account here:

https://cyphergoat.com/affiliate/register

Once registered, affiliates receive a referral link and access to the affiliate dashboard where they can monitor performance and earnings.

## **Responsible Promotion**

Affiliates are expected to promote CypherGoat responsibly.

This includes avoiding:
- misleading claims about anonymity
- guarantees about rates or execution
- deceptive marketing practices

Users should always understand what service they are using and what trade-offs exist.

Affiliates who violate these principles may have their accounts disabled.
