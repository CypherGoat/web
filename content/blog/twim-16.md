### Recent News

- Version v5.6.5 of Cake Wallet and the Monero.com wallets have been released. It includes bug fixes and improvements. [Release](https://github.com/cake-tech/cake_wallet/releases/tag/v5.6.5)
- Version v4.13 of P2Pool has been released by SChernykh. It includes connection improvements, bug fixes, and improvements. [Release](https://github.com/SChernykh/p2pool/releases/tag/v4.13)
- Version v0.9.3 of Monfluo has been released by acx. It includes various bug fixes, new features, and improvements. [Release](https://codeberg.org/acx/monfluo/releases/tag/0.9.3)
- A new European Monero pool EuroXMR has been launched. [Website](https://euroxmr.eu/)
- The next Cuprate meeting has been scheduled for January 6, 2025 at 1800 UTC. It will take place in the [Matrix](https://matrix.to/#/#cuprate:monero.social)/[IRC-Libera](irc://irc.libera.chat/#cuprate) chats, in the #cuprate channel.
